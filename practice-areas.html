Practice Areas

Comprehensive legal services across 14 areas of law — from arbitration and commercial litigation to corporate transactions and regulatory compliance.

Core Specialisations

Areas of Particular Depth

These four areas represent the deepest specialisations of our Founding Partner and senior counsel.

⚖

Arbitration & Dispute Resolution

Our practice covers the full spectrum of domestic and international arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, the ICA Rules, and UNCITRAL/ICC frameworks. We handle mediation, conciliation, enforcement and challenge of arbitral awards, and representation in arbitration petitions before the High Courts and the Supreme Court. Our team has extensive experience drafting arbitration clauses and representing PSUs and corporates — including Power Grid Corporation, NHAI, and the Government of NCT — in high-value proceedings.

⚖

Civil, Commercial & White-Collar Crime Litigation

We provide end-to-end representation across the Supreme Court of India, the High Court of Delhi, and all District Courts, as well as specialised tribunals including NCLT, DRT, DRAT, TDSAT, NGT, RERA, and the Central Administrative Tribunal. Our litigation practice covers commercial disputes, civil suits, writ petitions, and white-collar crime matters — including economic offences, corporate fraud, and cheque dishonour proceedings.

⚖

Insolvency & Bankruptcy

We advise creditors, debtors, resolution professionals, and other stakeholders under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, 2016. Our work spans distressed asset restructuring, debt resolution strategies, corporate insolvency resolution processes (CIRP), and representation before the NCLT and NCLAT. We provide practical guidance to both financial creditors and operational creditors navigating India's insolvency framework.

⚖

Cyber Law

Our Founding Partner holds a Certificate Course in Cyber Law from the Indian Law Institute (ILI), and our team advises on matters arising under the Information Technology Act, 2000, including cyber fraud, data breaches, online defamation, electronic evidence, and digital contract enforceability. We represent clients in cyber-crime proceedings before courts and regulatory authorities, and provide advisory services on cyber-risk mitigation and IT compliance.

01Arbitration & Dispute Resolution

Domestic arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, and ICA Rules; international arbitration under UNCITRAL and ICC frameworks; mediation and conciliation; enforcement and challenge of arbitral awards; representation before High Courts and the Supreme Court in arbitration petitions; drafting arbitration agreements and clauses; extensive experience representing PSUs and private companies in disputes involving Power Grid Corporation, NHAI, and the Government of NCT of Delhi.

02Banking, Finance & Insurance

+

Banking regulation and compliance, securitisation, project and acquisition finance, derivatives, corporate restructuring, loan and security documentation, proceedings under the SARFAESI Act before DRT and DRAT, RBI compliance advisory, and insurance and reinsurance coverage disputes.

03Broadcasting, Telecom & Media Laws

+

Regulatory advisory for broadcasters, Multi-System Operators (MSOs), DTH operators, and Internet Service Providers (ISPs); representation before the Telecom Disputes Settlement and Appellate Tribunal (TDSAT), High Courts, and the Supreme Court on regulatory, licensing, and broadcasting petitions.

04Competition Law

+

Representation before the Competition Commission of India (CCI) and Competition Appellate Tribunal (COMPAT); cartel and anti-trust investigations; abuse of dominance matters; merger control filings and compliance; internal competition compliance audits for corporates.

05Corporate & Commercial

+

Company incorporation, joint venture structuring, FDI and regulatory approvals, commercial contract drafting and negotiation, corporate due diligence, corporate restructuring, and Special Economic Zone (SEZ) advisory. We advise clients across all stages of the business lifecycle.

06Electricity & Energy Laws

+

Power project documentation including Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs), EPC and O&M contracts, and concession agreements; renewable energy regulatory advisory; representation before State Electricity Regulatory Commissions (SERCs), the Central Electricity Regulatory Commission (CERC), and the Appellate Tribunal for Electricity (APTEL).

07Environment Laws

+

Regulatory compliance advisory under the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, the Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, and the Environment Protection Act; environmental litigation before the National Green Tribunal (NGT), High Courts, and the Supreme Court of India.

08Intellectual Property Laws

+

Patents, trademarks, copyright, designs, and geographical indications; IP prosecution and registration; licensing and assignment agreements; enforcement and infringement actions; litigation before the Intellectual Property offices, the Intellectual Property Appellate Board (IPAB), and the courts.

09Insolvency & Bankruptcy

+

Comprehensive advisory under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, 2016; distressed asset restructuring; debt restructuring strategies; corporate insolvency resolution process (CIRP) representation; personal insolvency matters; representation before the NCLT and NCLAT for both financial and operational creditors.

10Labour & Employment Laws

+

Labour law compliance advisory for employers; industrial dispute resolution; drafting employment contracts, service rules, and HR policies; representation before Labour Courts, Industrial Tribunals, the High Court of Delhi, and the Supreme Court of India. We also advise on retrenchment, termination, disciplinary proceedings, and employment-related statutory compliance.

11Litigation — Civil, Commercial & Criminal

+

End-to-end representation before the Supreme Court of India, the High Court of Delhi, District Courts, and specialised tribunals including NCLT, DRT, DRAT, NGT, TDSAT, RERA, and the Central Administrative Tribunal (CAT). Our litigation practice includes white-collar crime, economic offences, cheque dishonour under Section 138, corporate fraud investigations, and criminal defence.

12Mergers & Acquisitions

+

Transaction structuring and deal negotiation; corporate and legal due diligence; SEBI Takeover Code compliance; transactional documentation including Share Purchase Agreements (SPAs), Shareholders' Agreements (SHAs), and Joint Venture Agreements; regulatory filings and approvals for M&A transactions.

13Property & Real Estate

+

Township and infrastructure project documentation; Real Estate Regulatory Authority (RERA) representation for developers and homebuyers; land acquisition advisory; joint development agreements; title due diligence; drafting sale deeds, lease deeds, and development agreements.

14Tax Litigation

+

Advisory and litigation in GST, customs, central excise, service tax, and VAT matters; representation before the Customs, Excise and Service Tax Appellate Tribunal (CESTAT), the Income Tax Appellate Tribunal (ITAT), the High Courts, and the Supreme Court of India.

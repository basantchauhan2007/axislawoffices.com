Our Story

From Chauhan Law Offices to Axis Law Offices

Axis Law Offices was founded by Sagar Chauhan, Advocate, and carries forward the legacy and track record of Chauhan Law Offices — a firm he established and built over a decade of rigorous legal practice in New Delhi. The rebrand to Axis Law Offices reflects the firm's growth, its expanding team of senior counsel, and its ambition to provide integrated, full-service legal representation across India.

The continuity of the firm's history, its client relationships, its courtroom experience, and its professional reputation are fully preserved. Every mandate won under the Chauhan Law Offices banner is part of the same team's story — now operating under the Axis Law Offices identity.

Today, the firm is led by Founding Partner Sagar Chauhan alongside Partners Rohit Kathuria, Dhruv Varma, and Sanjay Kumar Yadav — a team that collectively covers the full spectrum of civil, commercial, criminal, constitutional, and regulatory legal work.

Our Philosophy

How We Work

Axis Law Offices is a dynamic, full-service firm dedicated to providing clients the best possible legal solutions — individually tailored, reliably delivered, and grounded in deep subject-matter expertise.

👁

Attentive Listening

We invest time in understanding each client's specific business, structure, and objectives before charting a legal strategy. No two matters are treated alike.

🔒

Strict Confidentiality

Client information is held in the strictest confidence. Discretion is not an afterthought — it is a foundational commitment of everything we do.

⚖

Objective Problem-Solving

We advise on the law as it is, not as clients wish it to be. Accurate, honest counsel — even when difficult — serves our clients' long-term interests best.

📞

Prompt Communication

We keep clients informed at every stage — on case strategy, developments, and next steps — so they are never left wondering about the status of their matter.

🎯

Dependable & Flexible Service

Loyalty to Clients' Interests

Founding Partner

Founding Partner

Sagar Chauhan

Advocate

Sagar Chauhan is the Founding Partner of Axis Law Offices, with over ten years of experience in the legal profession. His practice is anchored in civil, commercial, and white-collar crime matters, with a particular specialisation in Alternate Dispute Resolution (ADR) — an area in which he has built a strong national reputation.

He advises and represents clients on matters spanning consumer laws, banking laws, insurance laws, commercial laws, civil laws, and insolvency and bankruptcy proceedings. He has handled complex, high-stakes commercial arbitrations on behalf of major Public Sector Undertakings including GAIL (India) Ltd., Power Grid Corporation of India Ltd., and Oil and Natural Gas Corporation — and is currently an empanelled advocate for Power Grid Corporation of India.

Sagar is well-versed in cyber law, holding a Certificate Course in Cyber Law from the Indian Law Institute (ILI). He has also provided contract management services to a multinational corporation, demonstrating fluency in the intersection of law and business operations.

He regularly appears before the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India and the High Court of Delhi, as well as before the Debt Recovery Tribunal (DRT), Debt Recovery Appellate Tribunal (DRAT), National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT), Telecom Disputes Settlement and Appellate Tribunal (TDSAT), and the National Green Tribunal (NGT).

His approach is client-centric, pragmatic, and solution-oriented — making him a trusted advisor in high-value and sensitive matters across industries.

Courts Supreme Court of India; High Court of Delhi; DRT; DRAT; NCLT; TDSAT; NGT
Education Certificate Course in Cyber Law, Indian Law Institute (ILI)
The Team

Our Partners

Rohit Kathuria

Partner

A graduate of Amity Law School, Noida, Rohit has been practising law since 2015. He has appeared before all forums and courts in Delhi NCR and gained deep knowledge of procedural law as a Legal Researcher for Hon'ble Justice Valmiki Mehta at the Delhi High Court.

He has worked with the Standing Counsel of the Government of NCT of Delhi, representing the Delhi Government in high-profile state matters before the Delhi High Court and the Supreme Court of India. He has also served as legal counsel with the Delhi High Court Arbitration Centre (DHCAC) and has been appointed Local Commissioner in various matters by the Delhi High Court.

Rohit holds an integrated B.Com LL.B (Hons.) degree and brings strong expertise in business and financial legal matters. He previously worked with O.P. Khaitan & Co. and Remfry & Sagar. He also holds a Diploma in Cyber Law and handles cyber-crime related matters.

Courts All forums in Delhi NCR; Delhi High Court; Supreme Court of India
Education B.Com LL.B (Hons.), Amity Law School; Diploma in Cyber Law

Dhruv Varma

Partner

A graduate of Amity Law School, Noida, Dhruv has been practising law since 2018 with extensive experience in commercial litigation and arbitration before various fora. His primary focus is dispute resolution in commercial, banking, electricity, and consumer matters.

He has handled disputes across diverse industry sectors involving arbitration law, contract law, and company law, and has conducted due diligence for banks, companies, commercial contracts, and arbitration agreements.

Prior to joining the firm, Dhruv worked with Madan & Associates and with Senior Advocate Sushil Kumar Jain, gaining rich experience under eminent judges and senior advocates of the Supreme Court of India and the High Court of Delhi.

Courts Supreme Court of India; High Court of Delhi; Commercial and arbitral tribunals
Education LL.B, Amity Law School, Noida

Sanjay Kumar Yadav

Partner

Sanjay Kumar Yadav has over five years of legal practice, primarily across writ jurisdiction, civil jurisdiction, criminal jurisdiction, consumer laws, service laws, and labour laws.

He currently handles consumer litigation, labour law matters, civil suits, and criminal proceedings across a wide range of forums in and around Delhi. He is empanelled with BSES Rajdhani Power Ltd. and Apollo Hospital (Sarita Vihar branch).

Courts High Court of Delhi; District Courts (Patiala House, Tis Hazari, Saket, Karkardooma, Dwarka, Rohini); NCLT; NCDRC/SCDRC; CAT; RERA
Panels BSES Rajdhani Power Ltd.; Apollo Hospital (Sarita Vihar)
